ABOUT US

We have all found ourselves, at least once, looking at a picture wandering were it was taken. Up until now no app is able to guess the location of an image based on image data. Here’s where we come in ! We are a group of five engineering students at Télécom SudParis who worked on the development of an image geoguessing AI that can tell you in which department your image was taken once you upload it into the website.


Geolocation can be defined as the identification or estimation of real-world geographic location using location based data.The location based data used for geolocation includes but is not limited to satellite images, sensor data and so on.

Humans are not the best at guessing locations based on sensor data. But when it comes to location based image data, well travelled humans perform fairly well at guessing the location of where the image was taken. With google street view, it is now possible to obtain image data from most location in France. The game Geoguessr from which our project found its inspiration, presents street view data to human players who attempt to guess the location of the street view image. That is precisely what we attempted doing, except there is only one player playing the game, our AI.
